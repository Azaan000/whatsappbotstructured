ONLINE MARRIAGE / ONLINE NIKAH

Procedure:
- At least one of the parties must be residing outside Pakistan.
- The legal process and documentation remain identical to a conventional Nikah.
- The only difference is that one party participates remotely through a secure online platform.

Time Required:
- The time required varies from case to case. Please consult our legal team for a more accurate estimate.

Required Documents (from both parties):
- Valid CNIC / NICOP or Passport
- Recent passport-size photographs
- 2 Witnesses (CNIC of both witnesses required)

COURT MARRIAGE
